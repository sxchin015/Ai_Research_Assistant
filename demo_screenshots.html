Explain how RAG works in simple terms
AI
🤖 Groq LLaMA
What is RAG?
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) connects an AI model to your documents so it answers from real content — not just its training data.

How it works in 3 steps:
1. Index — PDF is split into chunks, converted to number vectors (embeddings), stored in FAISS.
2. Retrieve — your question is also vectorised; FAISS finds the most similar chunks using cosine similarity.
3. Generate — the matching chunks + your question go to Groq LLaMA, which writes a grounded, accurate answer.
